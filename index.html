About

    The ToDo list is been created to note all the daily activities and to remember things in their daily life.
    It allows the user to enter their details and store their information and it also allows the user to delete and edit the data they enter.
    It list everything that you have to do, with the most important tasks at the top of the list, and the least important tasks at the bottom.
    By keeping such a list, you make sure that your tasks are written down all in one place so you don't forget anything important.
